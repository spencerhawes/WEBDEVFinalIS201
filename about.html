About Me

Life As Is

My name is Spencer Hawes. I was born and raised in Sandy, Utah and am currently attending Brigham Young University studying Neuroscience. Currently, I am planning on attending medical school after graduating from BYU and pursing a career in surgery.

On Februrary 17, 2023 I was married to Halle Young in the Draper Utah Temple. It has been the most important day of my life so far. I am so happy to be married and share my life with Halle.

I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. I served a mission in the Ecuador Quito North and Arizona Mesa Missions from August 2019 to December 2021.
